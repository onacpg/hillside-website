Privacy Policy

Last updated: July, 2026

Hillside Animal Clinic (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through our website, how we use it, and your choices regarding that information.

Information We Collect

We only collect personal information that you choose to provide. Specifically:

  • Newsletter signup: If you opt in to our email newsletter, we collect the email address you provide so we can send you clinic updates, promotions, and pet care information.
  • New client form: If you choose to complete our new client form, you may provide information such as your name, contact details, and pet information. This form may direct you to a third-party platform (see “Third-Party Services” below).

We do not collect personal information from you without your action or consent.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Send newsletters and clinic communications you have requested
  • Process and respond to new client inquiries
  • Provide and improve our services

We do not sell your personal information.

Cookies and Analytics

Our website may use cookies or similar technologies to understand how visitors use the site and to improve your experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Third-Party Services

Some features, such as our new client form or newsletter, may be operated by third-party providers. When you use these features, your information is handled according to that provider’s privacy policy. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you interact with.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information you provide. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

You may unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time using the link in any email. To request access to, correction of, or deletion of your information, contact us at email@email.com.

Children’s Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from them.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
