    Why Choose Us

    Why Hillside Animal Clinic?

    Proudly serving Chino Hills since 2005, Dr. Bryan H. Kim has been recognized by the Chino Hills community for excellence in veterinary medicine — combining compassionate care with advanced diagnostics and a team that truly listens.
    Hillside Animal Clinic is a doctor-owned and operated practice that combines advanced diagnostic technology with personalized care. Our veterinarians focus on the most effective and appropriate treatment options for your pet and your family—not simply the most expensive ones.
    At Hillside, your pet isn’t just a patient — they’re family. From the moment you walk through our doors, our team takes the time to comfort your companion, ease your worries, and make every visit feel warm and unhurried. Because when you love your pet, so do we.
    What We Offer

    Comprehensive Veterinary Services
    in Chino Hills, California

    From routine wellness visits and vaccinations to advanced surgery and international travel certificates — we offer the full spectrum of care your pet needs, all under one roof. Have any questions about our services? Give us a call today.

    General Care

    Wellness Exams

    Annual and bi-annual exams to keep your pet healthy at every life stage, from puppy to senior.

    Vaccinations

    Core and lifestyle vaccines tailored to your pet's age, health, and environment.

    Diagnostics & Imaging

    In-house bloodwork, urinalysis, digital X-ray, and ultrasound for fast, accurate results.

    Dental Care

    Professional cleanings, extractions, and oral assessments to prevent dental disease.

    Spay & Neuter

    Safe, routine procedures with attentive pre- and post-operative care.

    Senior & Chronic Condition Care

    Ongoing monitoring and management for aging pets and those living with conditions like arthritis, diabetes, or kidney disease.

    Specialty & Surgery

    Soft Tissue Surgery

    Routine to complex soft-tissue procedures performed in a sterile, monitored surgical suite.

    Orthopedic Surgery

    Fracture repair, ligament, and joint procedures to help restore mobility and comfort.

    Ultrasounds & Echocardiograms

    In-house ultrasound and cardiac imaging for accurate diagnosis of internal and heart conditions.

    USDA Travel Certificates

    International Travel

    USDA Pet Health Certification for Travel

    Planning to travel internationally with your pet? Our clinic is a USDA-accredited facility that issues official health certificates accepted worldwide. We guide you through every step — required vaccines, health assessments, and all country-specific documentation — so the process is seamless.
